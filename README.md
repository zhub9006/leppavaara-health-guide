# 🏥 Leppävaara Health & Wellness Resource Guide

A community resource guide for **Leppävaara residents** (Espoo, Finland), listing **health-related businesses, fitness facilities, health food options, and wellness services** — all mapped from verified OpenStreetMap data.

> **⚠️ Disclaimer:** All locations were collected from [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/) and verified as being located inside or immediately adjacent to the Leppävaara district boundary. **Always verify addresses, hours, and phone numbers directly with each business before visiting.**

---

## 📍 About Leppävaara

Leppävaara is a major district in **Espoo**, part of the Greater Helsinki region, with the postal code area **02600–02650**. It is well-served by public transport (Leppävaara railway station, regional buses) and is home to the **Sello** and **Galleria** shopping centres.

This guide covers health and fitness resources within the **Leppävaara core area and nearby sub-districts** (Pohjois-Leppävaara, Perkka, Kilonpuisto, Etelä-Leppävaara, Viherlaakso, Mankkaa, Kilo, Vallikallio).

## 📋 How to Use This Guide

1. **Pharmacies:** Sello-based pharmacies (Sellon Apteekki, Sanitum Apteekki) have extended weekday hours. Apteekki Elixir and Viherlaakson apteekki serve outlying parts of the district.
2. **Health centres:** **Leppävaaran terveysasema** (primary care) and **Leppävaaran neuvola** (maternal/child health) are at **2 Konstaapelinkatu, Vallikallio**. **Kilon terveysasema** (5 Trillakatu) serves the Kilo/Kilonpuisto area.
3. **Dentists:** Most are private; book via **Terveystalo** or **Mehiläinen**. Public dental care requires HUS appointments.
4. **Gyms:** **Elixia** offers two locations (Sello + Bertel Jungin aukio). **Fressi** has multiple locations with 24/7 access at Kilo. **Fitness24Seven** and **CrossFit Nuijala** cater to different training styles.
5. **Swimming:** Leppävaaran uimahalli has both indoor lanes + an outdoor maauimala in summer. Check espoo.fi for seasonal schedules.

## 🔍 Data Sources & Methodology

- All health and fitness locations were discovered via **OpenStreetMap** queries (amenity, healthcare, leisure, shop tags) within a ~2.5 km radius of the Leppävaara district centre (60.21967°N, 24.81414°E).
- Site names, addresses, hours, phone numbers, and websites were cross-referenced where available.
- Some entries may include data from adjacent sub-districts (Kilo, Viherlaakso, Mankkaa) since the residential area overlaps.
- **Verified live data sources:** OSM (points of interest tags), Sello centre directory, Espoo city services.
