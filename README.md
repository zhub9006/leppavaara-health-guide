## 🗺️ Quick Reference Map

All listed businesses are within the Leppävaara district and its immediate surroundings in Espoo, Finland. Most central locations cluster around:

- **Sello Shopping Centre** (Leppävaarankatu 3–9) — pharmacies, opticians, clinics, gyms, health food store
- **Hevosenkenkä / Panorama Tower area** — pharmacy, clinics, dental
- **Konstaapelinkatu** — public health station, neuvola
- **Veräjäpellonkatu** — swimming pool & sports hall complex
