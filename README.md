# 🏠 Leppävaara Health & Wellness Resource Guide

A community resource guide for **Leppävaara residents** (Espoo, Finland), listing **health-related businesses, fitness facilities, health food options, and wellness services** — all mapped from verified OpenStreetMap data.

---

## 📍 About Leppävaara

Leppävaara is a major district in **Espoo**, part of the Greater Helsinki region, with the postal code area **02600–02650**. It is well-served by public transport (Leppävaara railway station, regional buses) and is home to the **Sello** and **Galleria** shopping centres.

This guide covers health and fitness resources within the **Leppävaara core area and nearby sub-districts** (Pohjois-Leppävaara, Perkka, Kilonpuisto, Etelä-Leppävaara, Viherlaakso, Mankkaa).

> **Note:** All locations were collected from [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/) and verified as being located inside or immediately adjacent to the Leppävaara district boundary. **Always verify addresses, hours, and phone numbers directly with each business before visiting.**

## 📋 How to Use This Guide

1. **Pharmacies:** Sello-based pharmacies (Sellon Apteekki, Sanitum Apteekki) have extended weekday hours. Apteekki Elixir and Viherlaakson apteekki serve outlying parts of the district.
2. **Health centres:** **Leppävaaran terveysasema** (primary care) and **Leppävaaran neuvola** (maternal/child health) are at the same address on Konstaapelinkatu. **Kilon terveysasema** serves the Kilo/Kilonpuisto area.
3. **Gym/fitness:** Elixia is inside Sello (Level 3). Liikuntastudio Luhtavilla and Fressi are nearby residential studios.
4. **Swimming:** The only indoor swimming hall in Leppävaara is **Leppävaaran uimahalli** on Veräjäpellonkatu. Check espoo.fi for seasonal outdoor pools (maauimala).
5. **Health foods:** Visit **K-Citymarket Sello** or **K-Supermarket Galleria** for organic/bio (Pirkka brand) groceries.
6. **Dental care:** HUS dental care can be booked via huss.fi; private options at Terveystalo (Hevosenkenkä).
7. **Sports:** Seikkailupuisto Huippu (climbing park) is in Pohjois-Leppävaara; Boulderkeskus Konala and KiipeilyAreena Ristikko are slightly further in Konala.
