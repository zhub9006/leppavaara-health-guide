# 🏥 Leppävaara Health & Wellness Resource Guide

A community resource guide for **Leppävaara residents** (Espoo, Finland), listing health-related businesses and fitness facilities mapped from OpenStreetMap data within and around the Leppävaara area (postal codes 02600–02770).

---

## 📍 About Leppävaara

Leppävaara is a major district in Espoo, part of the Greater Helsinki region. It is well-served by public transport (Leppävaara railway station) and is home to the Sello and Galleria shopping centres. This guide covers health and fitness resources within approximately a **5 km radius** of the Leppävaara core.

> **Note:** All locations were mapped using [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/) data. Verify addresses, hours, and phone numbers directly with each business before visiting.

## 🚶 Getting Around

- **Bus:** Leppävaara bus station near the railway station — HSL regional buses
- **Train:** Leppävaara railway station for commuter rail (Helsinki–Espoo)
- **Car:** Sello and Galleria underground parking available
- **Walking:** Most health facilities are within a **10–15 minute walk** of each other around the Kamreerintie / Samarian terveyskeskus area
