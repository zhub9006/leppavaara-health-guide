## 📝 Notes & Disclaimers

1. **Data Currency:** OpenStreetMap data is community-maintained; verify opening hours before visiting.
2. **Hours & Contact:** Always check business websites or call ahead to confirm current hours.
3. **Emergency:** Dial **112** (Finland's emergency number).
4. **Health Centre:** Leppävaara residents register with their local **terveyskeskus** (health centre). Leppävaara is covered by **Lähilääkärit** and the public **Leppävaaran terveysasema**.
5. **Language:** Most health services in Espoo operate in Finnish and English.
