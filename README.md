## 🏥 Healthcare & Clinics

| Name | Type | Address |
|------|------|---------|
| **Leppävaaran terveysasema** | Primary Health Center | Konstaapelinkatu 2, 02650 Espoo |
| **Leppävaaran neuvola** | Maternal/Child Health Clinic | Konstaapelinkatu 2, 02650 Espoo |
| **Terveystalo Leppävaara hammaslääkäripalvelut** | Dental Clinic | Hevosenkenkä 3, 02650 Espoo |

### Primary Health Center
- **Leppävaaran terveysasema** – The main health center serving Leppävaara residents. Offers general practitioner services, nurse appointments, and preventive care.
- Located in the Vallikallio service area near the Sello shopping center.

### Children's Health Clinic
- **Leppävaaran neuvola** – Provides child health assessments, vaccinations, and parental support. Co-located with the primary health center.

### Dental Clinic
- **Terveystalo Leppävaara hammaslääkäripalvelut** – Dental services for the Leppävaara area. Part of the Terveystalo network.
- Located at Hevosenkenkä 3, inside the Ruusutorppa residential/commercial building.

A cluster of **outdoor fitness stations** is available near the Huippuvuori hill in Tali and along the Takti walking trail — featuring pull-up bars, horizontal ladders, rope climbs, battling ropes, and table tennis tables. These are free public installations, great for bodyweight training.
