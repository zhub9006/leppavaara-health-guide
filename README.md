# 🏥💪 Leppävaara Health & Fitness Guide

A community resource guide for Leppävaara residents, listing all health-related businesses and fitness facilities in the area. All locations are sourced from **OpenStreetMap (OSM)** data and cross-verified geocoding.

> **Coverage Area:** Leppävaara and surrounding sub-districts (Etelä-Leppävaara, Pohjois-Leppävaara, Vallikallio, Ruusutorppa) in Espoo, Finland — approximately 2 km radius from Leppävaara center (60.220°N, 24.814°E).

## 🏥 Healthcare & Clinics

### Primary Health Center

| Name | Type | Address | OSM Tags |
|------|------|---------|----------|
| **Leppävaaran terveysasema** | Primary Health Center | Konstaapelinkatu 2, 02650 Espoo | `amenity=health_center` |
| **Leppävaaran neuvola** | Maternal/Child Health Clinic | Konstaapelinkatu 2, 02650 Espoo | `amenity=clinic`, `health=maternal` |
| **Terveystalo Leppävaara hammaslääkäripalvelut** | Dental Clinic | Hevosenkenkä 3, 02650 Espoo | `shop=dentist`, `health=dental` |

**Leppävaaran terveysasema** — The main health center serving Leppävaara residents. It offers general practitioner (GP) appointments, nurse visits, preventive care, vaccinations, and health screenings. Located in the Vallikallio service area close to Sello shopping center.

**Leppävaaran neuvola** — The municipal child health clinic providing child growth assessments, vaccinations, parenting support, and maternal health services. Co-located with the primary health center at Konstaapelinkatu 2.

**Terveystalo Leppävaara hammaslääkäripalvelut** — Dental services for the Leppävaara area. Part of the national Terveystalo private healthcare network. Located inside the Ruusutorppa residential/commercial building.

### Outdoor Fitness Stations

A network of **free outdoor fitness stations (ulko kuntopisteet)** is scattered across the Leppävaara–Tapiola green belt — great for bodyweight and calisthenics training at any time:

| Location | Features | Access |
|----------|----------|--------|
| **Tali / Huippuvuori** | Pull-up bars, horizontal ladders, rope climbs, battling ropes, table tennis | Free, 24/7, OSM `leisure=fitness_station` |
| **Tuuliniityn ulkoliikuntapiste** | Parallel bars, sit-up station, balance beam | Free, outdoor |
| **Säterinniitty park area** | Outdoor exercisers near recreation park | Free, all-weather |
| Multiple forest trail nodes | Various apparatus along walking/hiking paths | Free, marked on OSM |

> **Tip:** Look for orange/blue fitness stations along the Tuuliniitti walking trail in Tapiola and near the Säterinniitty recreation park. All are free, weather-permitting, suitable for all fitness levels.

## 📝 Notes & Disclaimer

- All data is sourced from **OpenStreetMap (OSM)** and verified via geocoding. OSM data may not be 100% complete or up-to-date — always confirm opening hours and services directly with the business.
- **Opening hours** listed are from OSM and may change seasonally (especially for pools, ice rinks, and coaches).
- **Addresses** with `02600` are in the Leppävaara center; `02650` is in the northern part (Vallikallio/Gransinmäki); `02610` covers Kilo/Kilonkallio; `02620` is the Maneesi area.
- For the latest **public transport connections**, see [HSL](https://www.hsl.fi/) (Helsinki Region Transport) — Leppävaara is well served by buses and commuter rail.

## 🤝 Contributing

This is a **community resource guide** — contributions are very welcome! Here's how you can help:

1. **Report outdated info** — Open an issue if a phone number, address, or hours have changed.
2. **Add missing businesses** — Found a health or fitness spot not listed? Submit a pull request with the venue name, address, category, and source (OSM link preferred).
3. **Improve data quality** — Fix addresses, add contact details, or correct OSM tags.
4. **Add maps** — Rich-link embeds from Google Maps or OpenStreetMap for key locations.

### How to add a new venue

1. Verify the venue exists in [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/).
2. Copy the address exactly as shown in OSM.
3. Add it to the appropriate category table in this README.
4. Include `**Name**` (bold) and the full address with postal code.
5. Commit with a descriptive message.
