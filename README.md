# 🏥💪 Leppävaara Health & Fitness Guide

A community resource guide for Leppävaara residents listing all health-related businesses and fitness facilities in the area. All locations are based on OpenStreetMap data and cross-verified geocoding.

> **Coverage Area:** Leppävaara and surrounding sub-districts (Etelä-Leppävaara, Pohjois-Leppävaara, Vallikallio, Ruusutorppa, Tapiola, Matinkylä) in Espoo, Finland — approximately 3 km radius from Leppävaara center.

## 🏥 Healthcare & Clinics

### Primary Health Center
| Name | Type | Address |
|------|------|---------|
| **Leppävaaran terveysasema** | Primary Health Center | Konstaapelinkatu 2, 02650 Espoo |
| **Leppävaaran neuvola** | Maternal/Child Health Clinic | Konstaapelinkatu 2, 02650 Espoo |
| **Terveystalo Leppävaara hammaslääkäripalvelut** | Dental Clinic | Hevosenkenkä 3, 02650 Espoo |

**Primary Health Center:**
- **Leppävaaran terveysasema** — The main health center serving Leppävaara residents. Offers general practitioner services, nurse appointments, and preventive care. Located in the Vallikallio service area near the Sello shopping center.

**Children's Health Clinic:**
- **Leppävaaran neuvola** — Provides child health assessments, vaccinations, and parental support. Co-located with the primary health center.

**Dental Clinic:**
- **Terveystalo Leppävaara hammaslääkäripalvelut** — Dental services for the Leppävaara area. Part of the Terveystalo network. Located at Hevosenkenkä 3, inside the Ruusutorppa residential/commercial building near the health center.

## 📝 Notes & Disclaimer

- 📍 All locations are sourced from **OpenStreetMap** data plus cross-verified geocoding. Addresses and opening hours may change — verify before visiting.
- ⏰ Opening hours listed are approximate or the latest available from OSM; always check the facility's website or call ahead.
- 💰 Some sports halls require a day pass, sauna access cards, or club membership.
- 🎟️ Multi-sport passes: **Elixia**, **Liikuntastudio Luhtavilla**, and city of Espoo **liikuntalippu** card offer combined gym + swimming access.
- ℹ️ If you notice missing or outdated entries, please contribute by opening an issue or submitting a pull request on this repository.
- 🏥 The nearest university hospital (HUS) is in Helsinki; Leppävaara's health center handles primary care.
