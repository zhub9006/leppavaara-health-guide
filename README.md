# 🏥 Leppävaara Health & Fitness Guide

> A comprehensive community resource guide for **Leppävaara** residents (Espoo, Finland), listing health-related businesses, clinics, pharmacies, health food stores, gyms, sports halls, swimming pools, and wellness facilities in the area.

Found a missing listing or incorrect information? Please open an issue or submit a pull request to keep this guide up to date.

### Contribution Guide
1. Verify the business exists and the details are current
2. Add the entry in the appropriate category table
3. Include address, hours (if known), and contact info
4. Note the source of your information (e.g., OpenStreetMap, business website, phone call)

### Data Sources
- **OpenStreetMap** — primary source, verified via geocoding and map search
- **Local business directories** — e.g., pitajanmaenapteekki.fi, elixia.fi, mehilainen.fi
- **City of Espoo** — sporting facilities and ice rinks

2. **Hours & Contact:** Always check business websites or call ahead to confirm current hours.
3. **Emergency:** Dial **112** (Finland's emergency number).
4. **Health Centre:** Leppävaara residents register with their local **terveyskeskus** (health centre). Leppävaara is covered by **Lähilääkärit** and the public **Leppävaaran terveysasema**.
5. **Language:** Most health services in Espoo operate in Finnish and English.
