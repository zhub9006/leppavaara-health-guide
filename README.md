# 🏥 Leppävaara Health & Wellness Resource Guide

A community resource guide for Leppävaara residents, listing health-related businesses and fitness facilities in the Leppävaara area (Espoo, Finland). All locations were mapped using OpenStreetMap data.

---

## 📍 About Leppävaara

Leppävaara is a major district in Espoo, part of the Greater Helsinki region. It is well-served by public transport (Leppävaara railway station) and is home to the Sello and Galleria shopping centres. This guide covers health and fitness resources within approximately a 2–3 km radius of the Leppävaara core.

## 🗺️ Getting Around

- **Bus:** Leppävaara bus station near the railway station
- **Train:** Leppävaara railway station for commuter rail
- **Car:** Sello and Galleria underground parking
- **Walking:** Most businesses are within a 10–15 min walk of each other
